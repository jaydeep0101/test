About Product

Open a fully licensed bank account from your mobile phone in under 5 minutes, for free. Use the FIB mobile application to make and receive payments online and in person, send money to your loved ones locally and internationally, and much more. Now you can send money to your loved ones instantly, with full peace of mind that all of it has reached the right person. Sending money to another First Iraqi Bank user is free and instant.
